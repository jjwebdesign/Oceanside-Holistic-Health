About

Owned and operated by Miranda Cockayne, a certified Spa Practitioner, and certified Holistic Nutritionist. Oceanside Holistic Health and Bodywork has been operating in the Oceanside area of Vancouver Island since 2013 offering nutritional consultation and healthy cooking workshops; as well as massage and spa treatments.

What is Holistic Health?

Your body wants to be well and one of the most powerful ways to positively affect the health of your brain, gut, immune system, hormones, and body systems is through eating a nutrient dense diet and making healthy lifestyle choices. A Holistic Nutritionist and Health Coach can create personalized lifestyle and nutrition programs for people of all ages.

Nutritional Consultation for the Following Health and Lifestyle Challenges:

  • Healthy Eating for Diabetes Prevention and Maintenance
  • Nutrient Dense Foods for Boosted Immunity
  • Reversing Pre-Diabetes
  • Heart Health Diets
  • Specialized Diets such as Gluten Free, Vegetarian and Anti-Inflammatory Diets
  • Healthy Aging
  • Toddler Health and Feeding Picky Eater
  • Weight Loss
  • Sport and Fitness Nutrition
  • Diabetes and Heart Health Education
