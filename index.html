What is Holistic Health?

The Ideology of holistic health is based upon the premise that as a person we are interconnected with our mind, body and spirit, otherwise called our physical, mental, emotional and spiritual aspects. Holistic Health Practitioners combine several healing methods such as massage, nutrition, and healthy lifestyle coaching to promote overall balance to the natural rhythms of the body to achieve a state of health and wellness.

Oceanside Holistic Health is owned and operated by Miranda Lepe, Holistic Health Practitioner, certified Holistic Nutritionist and certified Massage Practitioner.

Miranda has had a passion for helping others achieve health and wellness for over 20 years. Oceanside Holistic Health (OHH) was created in 2008 to serve the Oceanside area of Vancouver Island with classes, workshops and lectures that focus on nutritious diets and healthy lifestyle topics. OHH has collaborated with numerous community and government organizations such as: Society of Organized Services; ACCRA; Nanaimo Regional District Parks and Recreation; School District 69; BCCDC and the BC Ministry of Health. OHH also works with individuals to create and implement personalized nutrition programs and healthy lifestyle habits with one on one consultations and personal wellness coaching.

What is Holistic Nutrition?

Your body wants to be well and one of the most powerful ways to positively affect the health of your brain, gut, immune system, hormones, and body systems is through eating a nutrient dense diet and making healthy lifestyle choices. OHH can create personalized lifestyle and nutrition programs for people of all ages.

Nutritional Consultation for the Following Health and Lifestyle Challenges:

  • Reversing type 2 Diabetes: Healthy Nutrient dense recipes and lifestyle coaching
  • Nutrient Dense Foods for Boosted Immunity
  • Reversing Pre-Diabetes
  • Heart Health Diets
  • Specialized Diets such as Gluten Free, Vegetarian and Anti-Inflammatory Diets
  • Healthy Aging
  • Toddler Health and Feeding Picky Eater
  • Weight Loss
  • Sport and Fitness Nutrition
  • Diabetes and Heart Health Education
